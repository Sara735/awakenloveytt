Lori Myles-Carullo

Lori’s love affair with yoga began twenty-two years ago in a gymnasium at McGill University. As a dancer and a very spiritual being in her essence, the movement, breath, and space that Yoga offered spoke to her soul and ignited a spark that has only gotten stronger and more powerful over the years.

Lori explored the styles of Integral, Sivananda, Iyengar, Ashtanga, Kundalini, and Kripalu Yoga, and loves to share a mix of all of these in her teaching. She took her first 200 hour Yoga Teacher Training Course at the Nosara Yoga Institute in Costa Rica in 2004, and that magical month changed her life forever in the best way. With twenty years of loving service in the healing arts, Lori brings a tremendous gift to the Yoga training through her expertise as a registered E-RYT 200 Yoga Instructor, a Massage Therapist, a Reiki Master, and a Psychotherapist with a Master’s Degree in Transpersonal Psychology. Lori also self-published her first book three years ago, entitled: ‘Beauty’s Way: Inspiration on the Path of Awakening’.

It is a true honor and a pleasure for Lori to be a guide for you in Italy, a land dear to her heart and soul, in this incredible month of training. She now lives most of the year in Costa Rica where her Italian husband is co-owner of a beautiful and magical retreat center called Mystica. You can find out more about Lori at www.lorimylescarullo.com

Hillary Faye

Hillary has been on a spiritual path for over fifteen years. She heals through her presence and open heart. Hillary has traveled the world on the path of healing, gaining a diverse prospective on life and spirituality that she brings into her trainings. Hillary Faye is an international Kundalini and Hatha Yoga Instructor, Meditation Guide, Healer and creator of the Awaken Love Workshop and Awaken Love Yoga Teacher Training. She is an E-RYT 500 and is over 600 hour-certified in Kundalini Yoga, Hatha Yoga, Pre-natal Yoga and Meditation.

Hillary has been teaching yoga for over 7 years around the world and has studied under many master yoga teachers including Gurmukh Kaur Khalsa. Hillary has donated her time teaching yoga and meditation to hundreds of homeless children in India and Bali. She is the director of Yoga Programming for the Youth Wellness Network which has brought yoga and wellness programs to over 50,000 youth in North America. She further spreads her message of love as a contributing blogger for and ambassador for Yogi Times.

Hillary’s goal is to help others expand their awareness so they awaken to the love that they are. The healing power of yoga and meditation has transformed her life and it is her passion to spread this healing to others.

Eat, Pray, and Love ~ All in One Location!

Terzo di Danciano is a charming 18th century villa located in the province of Arezzo in Tuscany, Italy. The main villa and adjoining outbuilding, near the lovely historic village of Cortona, and have a spectacular view overlooking a valley in the Tuscan hills. The outbuilding, called the ‘Bachificio’ will be our sacred Yoga space for the 28 days of training. Originally used as a work area for processing silkworms, this is the perfect location for supporting us during our powerful time of transformation. Traditionally, silkworms take 25 full days to grow from baby egg to exquisite butterfly – and the valuable silk comes from its cocoon. We will be blessed to draw on that rich energy of metamorphosis, purification, and glorious emerging.

Visit www.terzodidanciano.it for more information


Luca Francia, our host, is very happy to provide us with exquisite Italian cuisine made from 95% organic sources. We will be nourished and delighted in our taste buds through the month with treats such as freshly baked sourdough bread from the wood oven, fresh fruits and vegetables, extra virgin olive oil, and ricotta cheese made by local shepherds. Luca also honors all food allergies and sensitivities including gluten and dairy and creates alternatives that are equally divine.
